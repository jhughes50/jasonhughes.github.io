About Me

I am currently an Army Research Labs Journeyman fellow doing research in fully autonmous aerial vehichles, machine learning and optimization at the Robotics Research Center at the United States Military Academy at West Point. I recieved my Master's in Data Science at Fordham University where I also got my B.S. in Mathematics. I was a researcher in the Robotics and Computer Vision Lab as an undergrad. As a master's student I researched robust distributed optimal transport algorithms and their applications. My research interests lie in autonomous multi-agent systems and applications of machine learning and optimiation to such systems. I am also interested in green computing.
