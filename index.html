About Me

Jason Hughes is currently a Robotics Research Fellow supported by the ARL Journeyman Fellowship in the Robotics Research Center. His primary research interest is leveraging optimization and machine learning to built smarter and more robust multi-robot systems. He received my Master's in Data Science at Fordham University and his B.S. in Mathematics, also from Fordham. His previous research focused on developing robust and distributed optimal transport algorithms for resource and task allocation.
